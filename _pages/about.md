### Hi everyone!

I am a PhD candidate in economics at Università degli studi di Milano - Bicocca. 

My research interests include macroeconomics, monetary policies, heterogeneous agent models, financial frictions, and inequalities. 

My current research focuses on the effects of monetary policy in theoretical models that feature household heterogeneity and financial frictions under the supervision of Prof. Domenico Delli Gatti.
